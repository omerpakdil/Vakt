Prayer times look wrong

Check that location permission is enabled, your device time zone is correct, and your calculation method matches your local practice.

Reminders are not arriving

Open iOS Settings and confirm notifications are allowed for Vakt. Then check Prayer Reminders inside My Vakt.

Restore subscription

Open My Vakt and tap Restore. You can also manage subscriptions from your Apple ID subscription settings.

How does Saf presence work?

Saf presence is anonymous. It is meant to create a quiet feeling of standing alongside others without names, profiles, or visible identities.

Does Vakt upload my prayer reflections?

No. Prayer entries and reflection choices are designed to stay on your device.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions, and choose Vakt.
